Frequently Asked Questions

What is Opportunity App?

Opportunity App is a platform to connect students with organizations for various opportunities.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Create account" link in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form.

Who can use this platform?

The platform is designed for students looking for opportunities and organizations that want to provide them.

I forgot my password, what should I do?

On the login page, you can find a link to reset your password.
