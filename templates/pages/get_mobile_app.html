Get the Mobile App

Follow these steps to install our app on your mobile device for the best experience.

Step 1: Open on Your Phone

Open your phone's web browser and go to the following address:

{% url 'mobile_chat' as chat_url %}{{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}{{ chat_url }}

Step 2: Add to Home Screen

Once the page is open, your browser should give you an option to "Add to Home Screen".

For iOS (Safari)

  1. Tap the "Share" button at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  3. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner.

For Android (Chrome)

  1. Tap the three dots menu icon in the top-right corner.
  2. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
  3. Follow the on-screen prompts.

That's it! The app icon will now be on your home screen, giving you direct access to our chat feature.
